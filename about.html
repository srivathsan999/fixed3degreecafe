Sustainable Sourcing & Roasting Philosophy

Our coffee program is built on long-term relationships with farmers who prioritize regenerative agriculture and fair compensation. We cup every harvest, roast small batches in our Portland micro-roastery, and publish transparency reports twice a year.

We invest in compostable packaging, energy-efficient equipment, and donate 1% of revenue to watershed restoration projects in partner regions.

  • Direct trade with co-ops in Colombia, Rwanda, and Guatemala
  • Water-saving processing techniques and climate resilience funds
  • Roasting profiles tailored to highlight terroir and sweetness
